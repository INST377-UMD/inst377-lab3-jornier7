How to Play Rock-Paper-Scissors

  • Each player chooses either Rock, Paper, or Scissors at the same time.
  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock.
  • If both players choose the same option, it’s a tie.
  • Usually, players play best out of 3 or 5 rounds to decide the winner.
  • It’s a quick game of luck and strategy. Try to guess your opponent’s next move!
